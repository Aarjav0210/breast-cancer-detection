Who we are

At Femilina, we believe every women must know about breast cancer. In fact, 1 in every 8 women will be diagnosed with breast cancer at some point in her life. We know this can be intimidating and scary, but awareness is very important. Early detection can help to catch the diseases when it is most treatable, and it can help saves lives. Femilina will help you regain control of your feminine health, by detecting breast cancer using mammogram data. Click below to get started!


How we help

Femilina helps detect breast cancer, through the use of machine learning. Once the mammogram is uploaded, the ML algorithm will take in the data. Then, an output will be presented which detects and determines the type of tumor/cancer. It also depicts the properties that could have caused this concern. Please note that all of the data will remain confidential.

Additional Features


We want to empower women to take a stand for their health. Check out the "Support/Resources" tab where you can find out more about breast cancer as well as survivor stories. You can also find support groups to join as well as other services.
